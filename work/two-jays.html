Retail Static Site Performance

Two J's

A high-performance static site for a local retail business — engineered for fast load times, mobile-first experience, and a clean UI that converts.

Challenge

A local retail business losing customers to a slow, outdated site.

Two J's needed a web presence that could keep pace with the expectations of modern shoppers — fast, mobile-friendly, and visually credible. Slow load times and poor mobile experience were creating drop-off before customers even saw the product offering.

The brief was clear: build something fast, clean, and easy to maintain long-term without heavy infrastructure.

Approach

Static-first architecture for maximum performance.

The technical decision to go static was deliberate. No unnecessary server-side rendering, no bloated frameworks — just clean HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript deployed to a fast CDN. The result is near-instant load times regardless of device or connection quality.

Design-wise, the focus was conversion: clear product presentation, scannable layout, and prominent calls-to-action. Mobile was designed first, then scaled up — not the other way around.

Outcome

Fast, scalable, and easy to maintain.

Two J's now operates on a lean, high-performance web infrastructure with a site that loads fast on any device. The static architecture keeps ongoing costs minimal and the codebase easy to update as the business evolves.
